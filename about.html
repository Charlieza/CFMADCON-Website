Our Story

Established in 2022, CFM Advisory & Consulting was born at the crossroads of tax expertise and astronomical insight. Founded by a tax specialist and an astronomer, our firm brings together two disciplines that rarely intersect — but offer immense power when they do. Where tax regulation demands structure and compliance, astronomy brings systems thinking, data fluency, and a long-term perspective.

We launched CFMADCON to help individuals, businesses, and institutions move from reactive compliance to proactive, data-informed leadership. In an era defined by digital transformation and the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR), it’s not enough to stay legal — you must stay future-ready.

At our core, we believe tax is more than obligation — it’s an opportunity. Data is more than numbers — it’s intelligence. And development isn’t just a goal — it’s a measurable journey. We proudly serve changemakers, professionals, and mission-aligned institutions who know that smart compliance and scientific thinking are a powerful combination.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide innovative, data-driven tax and advisory solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and institutions to thrive in a digitally transforming world — while advancing compliance, strategic insight, and societal impact.

Our Vision

To be a leading African-born advisory firm that redefines tax practice through technology, big data, and development-focused innovation — bridging compliance with opportunity and empowering a new generation of professionals and changemakers.
